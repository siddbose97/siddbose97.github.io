Who is Siddhartha Bose?

Growing up in the UAE, Singapore, Bangladesh, Bahrain, and finally settling in Los Angeles, CA I was very fortunate to live a very global life. One thing that was always constant, however, was my love for learning and my passion for collaboration.

After my years at UCLA I spent a few months working at Darktrace, a cybersecurity company, before making my way back to Singapore to serve as a weapons technician in Singapore Armed Forces. My hope is to be back in the US pursuing a career in product development and software development in 2022. My areas of interest include Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Technology Management.
