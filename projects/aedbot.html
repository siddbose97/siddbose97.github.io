Mission Success, Safety Always!

AED Locator Bot

Misson Success, Safety Always! Posters all over my camp scream these words. A constant reminder that safety comes before all in the military. Since April 2020 I've been an Armament or Weapons technician in the Singapore Armed forces and the term does little to encapsulate the day-to-day work I do. I spent most of 2020 maintaining and repairing the FH2000 howitzers but then starting in 2021 I moved over to small arms maintenance. Although serving under the same battalion my job description shifted wildly. When I was working on the FH2000's, I would go to the same base every day and repair one of 21 guns housed within its garages. When I moved to small arms maintenance, I began travelling to every camp in Singapore to go and repair the weapons on site. Beyond the logistical aspect of traveling from camp to camp, the transient nature of my vocation brought about safety concerns.


Singapore is known for many things: the Marina Bay Sands, the Gardens by the Bay, and even the Merlion fountain. The only thing the island nation is more known for is its heat and humidity. Hovering around 86F year-round with over 80% relative humidity, heat injuries are rampant. Heatstroke can lead to the loss of consciousness and even death. As such, as a precautionary measure, all soldiers are CPR and AED trained. The issue is, however, that when we travel to different camps, we aren't always aware of where the nearest AED is. This is inherently a liability and as such, I decided to leverage Telegram Bot APIs once more to create a bot that solved the problem.


With help from officers around Singapore, I was able to gather the locations of every AED in every camp. Using this information, I created a bot that either served static maps with AED locations of a requested camp or took a user's live location and returned the location and directions of the nearest AEDs. Although the bot has yet to be needed in an emergency situation, it ensures that in such a situation, soldiers around the country have the information required to be safe.
